Projects where I have been involved

Enhancement of high spatial resolution AngioCT images using bilateral multiband filtering (2016)

My bachelor's degree final project

I made this project to get my Bachelor's in Biomedical Engineering for the Escuela de Ingeniería de Antioquia in Colombia (EIA University now). In this project, I used a filter called bilateral multiband to enhance high-resolution CT angiographic scans, Quantitative and qualitative analysis of filtering where performed.
Additionally it was implemented a 3D visualization algorithm (VTK) integrated on UI (Matlab).

Development of automatization system for a greenhouse of coffee (2017)

Work as independat contractor

This is a project from de los Andes Coperativa in Colombia which consists in building an automatic greenhouse of coffee, this is a social project to educate the community around the rural areas about the culture of coffee, we try to show them how with not very advanced technology we can also create a huge impact on crop efficiency.
Our responsibilities where the development of the control system for humidity and temperature of substratum. Additionally, the system is able to monitor meteorological conditions, save and send SMS reports and alerts.
