Projects where I have been involved

Enhancement of high spatial resolution AngioCT images using bilateral multiband filtering(2016)

My bachelor's degree final project

I made this project to get my Bachelor's in Biomedical Engineering for the Escuela de Ingeniería de Antioquia in Colombia (EIA University now). In this project I used a filter called bilateral multiband to enhance high resolution CT angiographic scans, additionally it was implemented a 3D visualization algorithm (VTK) integrated on UI (Matlab).
