  • Visualizing the smallest vessels in CT images requires the use of high spatial resolution CT scans (0.5mm/slice)
  • Due to ionizing limitations, these scans are often affected by high noise and low SNR.
  • We require post-processing techniques to improve high-resolution CT scans, reducing noise BUT not sacrificing spatial resolution.

 

 

Main goal

  • Develop a software tool that uses Bilateral Multiband Filter (BMF) to enhance quality of high-resolution AngioCT.

Specific goals:

  • Develop algorithm for BMF.
  • Evaluate filter performance, find “standard” parameters
  • Implement 3D visualization algorithm.
  • Identify clinical applications that could benefit from this filter.
